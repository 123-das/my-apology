FOR MY LOVE RUPSHIKHA

Everything I feel for you, everything – for my love, Rupshikha

You're bold, honest, and fearless – even when it makes things harder, you stay real.
You speak your mind, and somehow that’s what keeps me grounded.
You get angry, stubborn, and egoistic sometimes – but even then, I always see your love.
You stood by me when I couldn’t give you all you deserve. That loyalty means everything.
You understand me even when I struggle to explain myself properly.
Distance never made you distant – each short meeting feels like a whole new life.
You never left, even when you had reasons to. That’s a kind of love I can’t measure.
I know I am not perfect – I mess up and fall short – but you still choose me.
I am learning, working, and growing because you make me want to be better every day.
You are not just someone I love – you are the reason I try to be the best version of myself.
I know I haven’t been the perfect boyfriend. But I am trying, every day, in quiet ways you might not see. One day, I’ll give you everything you ever wished for, because you are the only one who never stopped believing in me.
